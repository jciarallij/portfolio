About Me

My name is Joshua David Ciaralli. I was born in a military family. My parents’ service in the United States Navy gave me the great opportunity to travel around the world. I have lived in California, Philippines, Alabama, Virginia, Japan, and now I currently live in Georgia in the North Atlanta area with my wife and two children.

I have always had an interest in technology, art and videogames. Through my younger years, I had a passion in creating things on the computer, from creating custom graphics in Photoshop or building 3d dimensional worlds in Modo and 3d studio max. Digital art isn’t my only interest in computers; I enjoy how they function, and I pride myself in being able to troubleshoot software and hardware issues.

Web development is now my outlet in technological creation. I completed a 16 week immersive full-stack web development course with DigitalCrafts, and learned many new skills. Combining my design ability with the structure of coding, the possibilities of what I can create seem limitless.

Check out this awesome article Hypepotumus wrote about me, a more in-depth look of my decision to get into development.
